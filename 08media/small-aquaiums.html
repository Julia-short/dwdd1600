15-30 Gallon Aquariums

With more space, we can now introduce greater variety into our aquarium setups. Even without considering new species, the larger volume allows for larger groups of the same fish. For example, while a 10-gallon tank can comfortably house about eight neon tetras, a 30-gallon tank can support a school of around 30. Additionally, the extra room allows for keeping multiple species together, rather than being limited to a single type of fish as is often necessary in smaller aquariums.
