Where can you buy

When it comes to buying fish, it takes a lot of effort. Knowing where to buy and what the benefits are takes time to know and understand, so here is a simplified chart of the pros and cons when it comes to the most common places to buy your fish and aquatic plants.

Store Pros Cons
Local Fish Store Knowledgeable staff, healthy fish, support local business Higher prices, limited selection
Chain Pet Store Wide selection, lower prices, convenient locations Less knowledgeable staff, variable fish health
Online Retailers Convenience, often lower prices, wide selection Shipping stress on fish, potential for poor quality
Aquarium Clubs/Forums Community support, often healthy fish from hobbyists Limited availability, may require travel or shipping
Local Breeders Healthy, well-cared-for fish, often unique varieties Limited availability, may not have all species

My Prefered Shop

When it comes to buying things I need for my aquarium, I go to the local chain pet store, as most of the items I require can be found there, such as food and chemicals. However, when it comes to buying fish, I prefer to purchase them from a local pet store called Animal Ark.

Animal Ark

Animal Ark is a local pet store that has been around for over 30 years. They have a wide selection of fish, plants, and aquarium supplies. The staff is knowledgeable and can help you find the right fish for your tank. They also have a great selection of freshwater and saltwater fish, as well as invertebrates and corals.
